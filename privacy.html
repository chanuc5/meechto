Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 07, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the CHANU mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). CHANU is a habit-tracking application designed to help users build and maintain positive routines.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App collects information to provide a personalized experience. This includes:

  • Habit Data: Names of habits, frequency, and completion logs created by you.
  • Personal Information: If you sign up, we may collect your name and email address to sync your data across devices.
  • Usage Information: We may collect non-personal data like device type and OS version to improve app stability.

2. Data Storage & Security

Your habit data is stored securely. We prioritize your privacy and take all necessary technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Note: We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal habit data to any third-party marketing companies.

3. Third-Party Services (Google Play)

The App may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. These may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (to track app performance)

4. "Never Undo" Policy

In line with our brand philosophy, Create Habits And Never Undo, the App provides reminders and notifications. You have full control over these notifications through the App settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
