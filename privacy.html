Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2026

Welcome to CHANU Habit Tracker. Your privacy is not just a feature; it's our core philosophy. We believe that your personal growth and daily habits should be witnessed by only one person: You.

1. Our Privacy Manifesto

CHANU is engineered as an offline-first application. Unlike traditional habit trackers, we do not require you to create an account, provide an email, or connect to the cloud. Your data is your property, and it stays on your device.

2. Data Encryption & Storage

All information—including habit names, streaks, and timestamps—is stored locally using secure Android storage systems (Room Database/SharedPreferences). We do not have any central server, meaning we physically cannot see, access, or sell your data.

3. Zero Third-Party Tracking

To ensure a pure and private experience, CHANU contains:

  • NO Third-party Advertisements (No AdMob)
  • NO Hidden Analytics (No Google Firebase/Flurry)
  • NO Social Media SDKs (No Facebook/Meta tracking)

4. Permissions Policy

We only ask for essential system permissions required for the app to function (like Notifications for your habit reminders). We will never ask for sensitive access like your GPS location, Contacts, or Microphone.

5. Data Deletion & Control

Since all data is local, you have absolute control. If you clear the app cache or uninstall the application, all your data is permanently wiped from your device. We do not keep any hidden backups.

6. Children’s Online Privacy

CHANU does not collect any data, making it safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We strictly comply with global privacy standards by maintaining a zero-data-collection environment.
