Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

At CHANU Habit Tracker, accessible from the Google Play Store, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by CHANU Habit Tracker and how we use it.

1. Information Collection and Use

CHANU Habit Tracker is an offline-first application. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII), habit data, or usage statistics to any external servers or third parties.

All data you enter into the app, such as habit names and completion status, is stored strictly on your local device using SharedPreferences. This ensures that your personal growth journey remains private to you.

2. Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app, we do not collect data and information on your phone. We do not use crash reporting tools that send your device data to us.

3. Third-Party Access

Since the app is offline and does not require an internet connection for its core features, no third-party companies or individuals have access to your data. We do not show ads (AdMob) or use any third-party analytics (Firebase) in the current version of the app.

4. Permissions

The app does not require any sensitive permissions such as Camera, Microphone, or Location. It operates with minimal system requirements to ensure your privacy is maintained.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, we ensure a safe environment for all users.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
